Mehrangarh Fort, Jodhpur

Also known as Mehran Fort, the Mehrangarh Fort in Jaipur is one of the most famous ancient places in India. Built by Rao Jodha, the founder of Jodhpur, in 1459, the fort is also one of the largest in the country, and is situated atop a hill, at an elevation of over 400 feet.

Meenakshi Amman Temple, Madurai

Built in the Dravidian style of architecture, the Meenakshi Amman Temple ranks as one of the most beautiful ancient places in India. Built in the early 17th century, the temple is located on the southern banks of the Vaigai river in Madurai, Tamil Nadu.

Golden Temple, Punjab

Also known as Sri Harmandir Sahib, the Golden temple is the most important religious site for the Sikhs, and celebrates humanity, tranquillity and brotherhood. Spread across two storeys, the gurdwara has a unique domed architecture, with the dome-part of it completely covered in pure gold.
